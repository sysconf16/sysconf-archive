How to Install & Configure Microsoft Edge

Microsoft Edge is a fork of Chromium and the new default browser in Windows 10 21H1+ and Windows 11. It's not recommended unless you're running Windows 10 or 11.

👍 Advantages

  • Good integration with Windows and other Microsoft applications.
  • Almost as good performance and battery life as Ungoogled Chromium.
  • Only browser with Play Ready DRM support. Also support Widevine DRM.
  • Auto-updates.
  • Supports direct installation of extensions from the Chrome Web Store.
  • Supports spell check and translate.

👎 Disadvantages

  • Translate not as good as Google Chrome.
  • Severely riddles with Microsoft spyware, adware and other unwanted garbage.
  • Awful to use out-of-the-box.


📥 Download & Installation

  1. See the start menu to find the "Microsoft Edge" shortcut.
  2. If the shortcut isn't found, visit this link to install the browser manually.


⚙️ Basic Configuration & Flags

🧰 Configuring the Settings

  1. Open Edge, skip through the introductory setup and wait for the new tab page to load.
  2. Click the cog in the top right and select "Custom" as the "Page layout".
  3. Under "Custom", turn off "Show quick links", "Background" and "Content".
  4. Click the 3 dots in the top right of the screen, then click "Settings".
  5. In the "Profiles" tab, ensure everything is disabled under "Sync", "Personal info", "Passwords", "Payment info" and "Profile preferences".
  6. In the "Privacy, search and services" category, enable "Strict" tracking prvention.
  7. In the "Privacy, search and services" category under the "Privacy" heading disable "Allow sites to check whether you have payment methods saved".
  8. In the "Privacy, search and services" category, disable "Required diagnostic data", "Optional diagnostic data" and "Personalise your web experience".
  9. In the "Privacy, search and services" category under the "Services" heading, disable all available options.
  10. In the "Privacy, search and services" category under the "Services" heading, click "Address bar and search".
  11. Under "Address bar and search", disable "Show me search and site suggestions using my typed characters" and "Show me suggestions from history, favourites and other data on this device using my typed characters".
  12. Under "Address bar and search", change "Search engine used in the address bar" from Bing to the search engine of your choice and "Search on new tabs uses search box or address bar" to "Address bar".
  13. Under "Address bar and search", click "Manage search engines", right click the search engine of your choice and click "Make default".
  14. In the "Start, home and new tabs" category, disable "Show home button on the toolbar".
  15. In the "Cookies and site permissions" category, disable/deny everything under "All permissions". Do NOT make changes to "Protected content" and "Application links" however.
  16. In the "Languages" category, disable "Offer to translate pages that aren't in a language I read" and "Enable spellcheck".
  17. In the "System" category, enable "Save resources with sleeping tabs" and change "Put inactive tabs to sleep after the specified amount of time" to "5 minutes of inactivity".
  18. Click the 3 dots in the top right of the screen, then click "Extensions".
  19. Enable "Allow extensions from other stores".

🚩 Tweaking the Flags

  1. In the address bar, type "edge://flags".
  2. In the search box, enter "#disable-search-engine-collection" and change to "Enabled" using the drop-down toggle.
  3. In the search box, enter "#fingerprinting-client-rects-noise" and change to "Enabled" using the drop-down toggle.
  4. In the search box, enter "#fingerprinting-canvas-measuretext-noise" and change to "Enabled" using the drop-down toggle.
  5. In the search box, enter "#fingerprinting-canvas-image-data-noise" and change to "Enabled" using the drop-down toggle.
  6. In the search box, enter "#hardware-media-key-handling" and change to "Disabled" using the drop-down toggle.
  7. Restart the browser.


🧩 Extension Setup

🛡️ uBlock Origin

  1. Click this link and click "Add to Chrome" to install.
  2. Restart the browser, click the puzzle piece in the top right and click "Manage extensions".
  3. Click "Details" underneath and ensure "Allow in Incognito" is enabled.
  4. Click "Extension options".
  5. In the "Settings" tab, ensure that only "Hide placeholders of blocked elements", "Show the number of blocked requests on the icon" and "Disable tooltips" are enabled.
  6. Under "Privacy" in the "Settings" tab, ensure that all options are enabled.
  7. In the "Filter lists" tab, only enable the "Built-in", "Ads", "Malware domains" and "Annoyances" lists.
  8. Click "Apply changes" and "Update now" in the "Filter lists" tab.
  9. Restart the browser.

❓ Extensions Not Listed

  1. Find your desired extension on the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" and install.
