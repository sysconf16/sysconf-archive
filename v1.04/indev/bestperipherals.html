ğŸ“ Notes for Headphones & IEMs

Bluetooth, BOSE, etc. headphones are NOT recommended because:

  • Significantly inferior audio reproduction and accuracy compared to the options listed.

Audio-Technica ATH-M50xs are NOT recommended because in comparison to DT 770s, they have:

  • Slightly more V-shaped (coloured) frequency response and reduced soundstage.
  • Less comfortable pads and worse isolation.
  • The cable is detachable however, unlike DT 770s.

Schiit Magni 3+ is NOT recommended because despite almost double the output power in comparison to the Atom AMP+, it:

  • Distorts at high volumes and has greater noise output.

ğŸ“ Notes for Speakers

Bluetooth, Logitech, etc. speakers are NOT recommended because:

  • Significantly inferior audio reproduction and accuracy compared to the options listed.

M-Audio, Yamaha and KRK studio monitors are NOT recommended because:

  • Perform worse than similarly priced competitors.

ADAM's T series studio monitors may be worth considering, but:

  • T5Vs perform inferiorly to JBL's 305p MKIIs, while being at a higher price.
  • T8Vs have a rear port which makes room placement more difficult, which competitors do not.
  • T7Vs are the only option worth considering.
